Rationale

The previous existing COMP 1950 website was brief, but to the point and gets the job done providing BCIT students with weekly lecture notes, example, and assignments. Considering creating a more user-friendly interface design in focus on the user experience and interaction where lecture notes, examples and other information are easily accessed. We have established new grounds on the redesign of the COMP1950 web site.

Site Layout

The Audience

Targeted audiences are all BCIT students. For students who are currently enrolled in BCIT COMP 1950- Intermediate Web Development and Design. This web site will provide an easy access to lecture notes, assignments, and exercises in stride towards their career and academic future. Alumni students may gain access to review previous material while prospective students could have an overview or syllabus of the course potentials.
